TinasPhoto

- Website

"tinasphoto.dk" is a website made towards my moms’ hobby as a photographer. Her pictures have caught the attention of many, both genuine folks, but also other photographers, so she thought a website would be a good idea. The website is made with the thought of being user-friendly and having a simple design that doesn’t catch the users’ attention as well. This was my first website project and I think we can all agree that the final product could be improved. It was also the first project made for a customer, which means all the decisions wasn’t entirely made by me, but first judged by my mom before it was implemented.
